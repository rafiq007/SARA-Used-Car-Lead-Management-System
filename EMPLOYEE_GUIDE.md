# 📱 Employee Workflow Guide - AutoDrive Pro Malaysia

## 🎯 Your Role as an Employee

As an employee, you are responsible for:
1. Creating new leads from TikTok inquiries
2. Managing and following up on your assigned leads
3. Updating lead status as the process progresses
4. Adding notes and progress updates
5. Submitting completed applications

---

## 🚀 Step-by-Step Workflow

### Step 1: Create New Lead (from TikTok Inquiry)

When a customer contacts you through TikTok:

1. **Login** to the system
2. Click **"Lead Pipeline"** in the sidebar
3. Click **"Add New Lead"** button
4. Fill in all required information:

   **Client Information:**
   - Full Name
   - Phone Number
   - Home Address

   **Lead Source:**
   - TikTok Video URL (copy from TikTok)
   - Assigned Employee (select yourself)

   **Vehicle Details:**
   - Vehicle Model (e.g., Honda Civic, Toyota Vios)
   - Year
   - Listing Price

   **Financial Profile:**
   - Monthly Basic Salary
   - Fixed Allowances (if any)
   - Yearly Bonus Average

   **Status:**
   - Set to "New" for fresh leads
   - Add initial notes if needed

5. Click **"Add Lead"**

> **💡 Tip:** The system will automatically calculate the DSR (Debt Service Ratio) and flag high-risk applications in red!

---

### Step 2: Track Your Leads

**View Your Leads:**
- Go to **"Lead Pipeline"**
- You will see ONLY the leads assigned to you
- Use the search box to find specific leads quickly

**Understanding Lead Statuses:**

| Status | When to Use |
|--------|-------------|
| **New** | Just created, not yet contacted |
| **In Progress** | Actively working on it, gathering documents |
| **Pending** | Waiting for customer action (e.g., pending documents) |
| **Submitted** | Application submitted to bank/manager |
| **Completed** | Successfully closed the deal ✅ |
| **Rejected** | Application declined or customer withdrew ❌ |

**Color Indicators:**
- 🔴 **Red Background** = High Risk (DSR > 30%) - Be careful!
- 🟡 **Orange Background** = Medium Risk (DSR 20-30%)
- 🟢 **Green Background** = Low Risk (DSR < 20%) - Good to go!

---

### Step 3: Edit & Update Lead Status

As you progress with the customer:

1. Find your lead in the **Lead Pipeline**
2. Click **"Edit"** button
3. Update any information that changed
4. **Change the Lead Status** to reflect current progress:
   - Customer sent IC copy? → Change to **"In Progress"**
   - Waiting for payslip? → Change to **"Pending"**
   - Sent to bank? → Change to **"Submitted"**
   - Deal closed? → Change to **"Completed"** 🎉
5. Click **"Update Lead"**

---

### Step 4: Add Follow-up Notes

**Keep a detailed log of every interaction!**

1. In the **Lead Pipeline**, click **"Log"** button (📝) next to the lead
2. A popup window will appear
3. Type your note in the text area, for example:
   - "Called customer, requested IC and payslip"
   - "Sent payslip to Maybank"
   - "Customer confirmed interest, will send documents tomorrow"
   - "Received all documents, submitting to bank"
   - "Bank approved loan!"
4. Click **"Add Entry"**
5. Your note will be saved with timestamp and your name

**Why Add Notes?**
- ✅ Track communication history
- ✅ Remember what was discussed
- ✅ Share progress with managers
- ✅ Prove your work and follow-ups

---

### Step 5: Submit for Approval

When ready to submit:

1. Edit the lead
2. Ensure all information is complete and accurate
3. Change status to **"Submitted"**
4. Add a final note: "Application submitted to [Bank Name] on [Date]"
5. Update the lead

Your manager/HR will now see this in their pending queue!

## ⚠️ Important DSR Information

**What is DSR?**
DSR (Debt Service Ratio) = Monthly Car Payment ÷ Monthly Income × 100

**Risk Levels:**
- 🟢 **0-20%** = Low Risk - Likely to be approved
- 🟡 **20-30%** = Medium Risk - May need extra documents
- 🔴 **30%+** = High Risk - Difficult to approve

**Tips for High Risk Leads:**
1. Double-check all salary information
2. Include ALL allowances and bonuses
3. Consider a cheaper vehicle
4. Suggest larger down payment
5. Add a guarantor if possible

## 💬 Common Scenarios

### Scenario 1: Customer Wants Different Car
1. Edit the lead
2. Change vehicle model, year, and price
3. System recalculates DSR automatically
4. Add note: "Customer changed preference to [new car]"
5. Update lead

### Scenario 2: Waiting for Customer Documents
1. Change status to **"Pending"**
2. Add note: "Waiting for customer to send payslip - due by [date]"
3. Set personal reminder to follow up

### Scenario 3: Customer Not Responding
1. Add note each time you try to contact
2. After 3 attempts with no response, add note: "Customer unresponsive"
3. Discuss with manager whether to mark as "Rejected"

### Scenario 4: Application Approved!
1. Change status to **"Completed"** ✅
2. Add note: "Loan approved by [Bank Name] on [Date]"
3. Celebrate! 🎉

## 🆘 Need Help?

**Can't see your leads?**
- Make sure you selected yourself as "Assigned Employee"
- Ask HR/Admin to assign leads to you

**DSR calculation seems wrong?**
- Double-check salary and allowances
- Make sure listing price is correct
- Refresh the page

**Forgot to add a note?**
- You can add notes anytime - just click "Log" button
- Notes are sorted by newest first

**Made a mistake?**
- Click "Edit" and correct the information
- Add a note explaining what was corrected
